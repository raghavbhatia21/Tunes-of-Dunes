Why Choose Tunes of Dunes for Jaisalmer Tourism?

Tunes of Dunes extends to you a warm welcome and is the partner you can rely on while undertaking a Jaisalmer tourism venture. We specialize in fashioning exclusive Jaisalmer tour packages that deliver the perfect blend of adventure, culture and luxury. Whether it is a family holiday that you are planning, a honeymoon trip you are going to or a solo expedition that you are merely carrying out, our tailor-made trip plans will enable you to have the maximum fun of the Golden City.

Experience the Best Desert Safari in Jaisalmer

If a person visits Rajasthan but does not experience the giant Thar Desert even once, then no one can say that the person has really been to Rajasthan. We have Desert Safari Jaisalmer packages with well-known brands available at the popular Sam Sand Dunes that will give you a feeling as if you have actually lived in the desert. You may choose a crazy Jeep Safari to go off-road in a 4x4 amongst the dunes or a silent Camel Safari to watch the mesmerizing sunset going down behind the horizon.

Luxury Camping & Cultural Evenings

Put up with the best Luxury Desert Camps in Jaisalmer which are full of all the latest comforts and traditional hospitality. We will entertain you with colourful Rajasthani folk dance and music, fire shows, and a delicious buffet dinner under the starlit sky as part of our evening packages. We make sure that your stay in Jaisalmer is a memorable one.

Top Things to Do in Jaisalmer

Our trips are not limited to just the sand dunes; we also take you to see all the other major attractions. Walk through the vibrant Jaisalmer Fort (Sonar Quila), be amazed by the detailed architecture of Patwon Ki Haveli, go to the ghost village of Kuldhara and have fun boating at Gadisar Lake. Tunes of Dunes offers you the best and cheapest sightseeing packages to Jaisalmer that cover the maximum places.
