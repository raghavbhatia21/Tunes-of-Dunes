## Patwon ki Haveli

Patwon ki Haveli stands as a major architectural masterpiece in Jaisalmer, truly reflecting the grandeur of Rajasthan's royal heritage and artistic brilliance. At the core of the Golden City, this set of five havelis symbolises a timeless piece of the wealth, culture, and detailed handiwork.



The travellers who delve into Jaisalmers' heritage often place this monument on their list of must-visit places. Tunes Of Dunes, on the other hand, makes sure visitors get to feel the magic of this place in the most engaging manner.
