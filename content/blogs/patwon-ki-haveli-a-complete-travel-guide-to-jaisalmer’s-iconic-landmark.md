## Everything about Patwon ki Haveli

Patwon ki Haveli stands as a showpiece among the architectural wonders of Jaisalmer. It portrays the richness of the art and culture of Rajasthan. Situated right in the heart of the Golden City, it is a group of five havelis that signify the lifestyle, culture, and wealth of that time through its intricate work.



Those who are interested in knowing about the history of Jaisalmer generally take Patwon ki Haveli as one of the primary attractions of the city. Tunes Of Dunes makes sure that the visitors get the most out of their visit and experience the real essence of the place.



Guman Chand Patwa, a wealthy businessman, and his sons were the people behind the construction of Patwon ki Haveli in the beginning of the nineteenth century. Patwon ki Haveli was set in five separate portions which were meant to be the residences for different members of the family.



This was the reason why the outward appearance of the building was so grand and each of the five parts had an elaborate design to it. The faade of the building is made of yellow sandstone and it is decorated with exquisite sculptures, jharokhas, balconies, and arches revealing the excellent talents of the local craftsmen.



Each part of the structure speaks of the trade, affluence, and Jain mercantile lifestyle. Jain traders were the ones who prospered in the region during those times.
