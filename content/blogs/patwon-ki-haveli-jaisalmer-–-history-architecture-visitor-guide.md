## Introduction to Patwon ki Haveli, Jaisalmer

Patwon ki Haveli is a prime example of Jaisalmer's luxurious architecture and a perfect reflection of the heyday of the Rajasthani culture and artistic brilliance. It is a group of five havelis, located in the center of the Golden City. They are a great legacy of prosperity, culture, and fine artistry of the past times. Trekkers who are interested in the history of Jaisalmer can't help but add this architectural marvel to their itinerary and Tunes Of Dunes promises to deliver that wonderful encounter.

## History of Patwon ki Haveli

Inception dates back to the early 19th century when the affluent merchant Guman Chand Patwa and his sons built the Patwon ki Haveli to openly flaunt their wealth, status, and artistic taste. Contrary to the great many havelis of Rajasthan, it was raised over a very long time thus it is extremely detailed. The picturesque yellow sandstone exterior features very intricate carvings, balconies, jharokhas, and arches that are the evidence of the high level of skills of the local craftsmen. The trading, wealth, and way of life of the Jain merchants who dominated the region are the subject of each piece of the haveli.

## Architectural Brilliance of Patwon ki Haveli

Upon stepping in, visitors are welcomed by vividly painted walls, mirrors decorating the walls, intricately carved ceilings, and old relics that reveal how the royals lived. One part of the whole set up is still under the Archaeological Survey of India, whereas the other part has been turned into a museum that exhibits old utensils, costumes, manuscripts, and furniture. Venturing through these areas gives tourists not only the knowledge of the mercantile past of Jaisalmer but also the architectural genius, thus making Patwon ki Haveli a cultural gem.

Thanks to the havelis prime location, it can be reached without any issues from Jaisalmer Fort and thus tourists can mix different heritage experiences of the same day at the primetime of their choice. It is very beneficial for the visitors who use their cameras that the sandstone shines with a lovely glow of the sun when it is either early morning or late afternoon giving the visitors of Tunes Of Dunes the option of guided tours which uncover hidden elements and captivating tales that are usually overlooked during solo visits.

## Inside Patwon ki Haveli: Art, Rooms & Museums

The interiors of Patwon ki Haveli are no less spectacular. One can come across gorgeous frescoes, intricate mirror works, carved ceilings, and exquisite passageways while walking through the haveli. Certain sections of the haveli are converted into museums under the supervision of the Archaeological Survey of India, where one can see the collection of ancient artifacts, manuscripts, utensils, and old, style garments which make the history feel real.

## Why Patwon ki Haveli Is Famous

Patwon ki Haveli is essentially distinguished by the immense hard work and intricate detailing that were the components of the construction.

When you move around the complex, you can definitely feel that a mere residential space couldn't have been the source of the same but rather a display of wealth and pride. The blend of Rajputana regal with sleek Mughal overlays is what makes it the most magnificent haveli in Jaisalmer.

It is hugely popular with tourists, as it is located near Jaisalmer Fortan only few minutes' walkthus making it a very feasible and worthwhile spot for anyone to stop and check out the old town.
