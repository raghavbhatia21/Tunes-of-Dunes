Desert Festival Jaisalmer

The Desert Festival Jaisalmer 2026 will be one more time the Golden City turning into a lively festival of the culture, heritage and desert life of Rajasthan. Commonly called Maru Mahotsav 2026, the great festival is a magnet for travellers, photographers, and culture enthusiasts from all over India and the globe. The festival set in the dunes and the ancient forts of Jaisalmer gives a once, in, a, lifetime experience of the Thar Desert's soul.

Fun with Culture!

At the Desert Festival Jaisalmer 2026, the whole city will be full of traditional folk music, Kalbeliya dance performances, puppet shows and vibrant processions. Artists in regal Rajasthani costumes display age, old heritage, and the residents, with pride, exhibit their traditions.

Maru Mahotsav 2026 is filled with fun and games from camel polo and camel races to turban tying competitions and moustache contests, and the excitement is guaranteed to be non-stop.

One of the grand events of Maru Mahotsav 2026 has to be the stunning landscape of Jaisalmer Fort and Sam Sand Dunes.

Sunset brings about the cultural nights where the desert is decorated with beautiful music, fire dances, and starry skies. Desert Festival Jaisalmer 2026 visitors often claim that it is an on-the-border experience with tradition and adventure.

Food and Local Market

Food lovers attending the Desert Festival Jaisalmer 2026 can indulge in authentic Rajasthani cuisine, from dal baati churma to ker sangri, prepared using traditional methods. Handicraft stalls, local jewellery, and embroidered textiles make Maru Mahotsav 2026 a paradise for shoppers as well.
