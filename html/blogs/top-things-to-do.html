Top 10 Things to Do in Jaisalmer

7 Min Read  |  Updated: Dec 2025

Welcome to Jaisalmer, the Golden City of Rajasthan, where every shimmering sandstone wall narrates a tale of valor and heritage. If you are planning an escape to this desert paradise, you might be wondering about the best Things to do in Jaisalmer to make your trip truly unforgettable. At Tunes of Dunes, we curate experiences that blend history, adventure, and local culture, ensuring you see the very best of Jaisalmer.

Marvel at the Living Fort of Jaisalmer

One of the most iconic Things to do in Jaisalmer is exploring the legendary Jaisalmer Fort, also known as Sonar Quila. Unlike most forts, this is a "living fort" inhabited by thousands of people. Walking through its narrow alleys, visiting the ancient Jain Temples, and looking out from the bastions as the sun sets are essential experiences. Tunes of Dunes recommends a guided heritage walk to truly appreciate the intricate architecture and the vibrant local life thriving within the fort's golden walls.

Adventure in the Sam Sand Dunes

For those seeking a thrill, head straight to the Sam Sand Dunes for a heart-pounding desert experience. High on the list of Things to do in Jaisalmer is a 4x4 Jeep Safari followed by a traditional Camel Ride. At Tunes of Dunes, we specialize in desert adventures that take you deep into the dunes, away from the crowds, where you can witness the raw beauty of the Thar. After the adventure, enjoy a cultural evening with folk music and Kalbeliya dance under the starlit sky, making it a highlight of your desert journey.

Discover History and Hauntings

Jaisalmer’s charm extends beyond the fort and dunes. Exploring the cluster of Patwon Ki Haveli is one of the most culturally enriching Things to do in Jaisalmer. The fine stone carvings and gold-painted ceilings are a testament to the city's rich merchant history. For the brave-hearted, a visit to the abandoned village of Kuldhara is a must. Legends say the village was cursed and deserted overnight, leaving behind ghostly remains that are fascinating to explore with Tunes of Dunes' expert storytelling guides.

Peaceful Moments at Gadisar Lake

To balance the adventure, one of the most tranquil Things to do in Jaisalmer is spent at Gadisar Lake. This man-made reservoir, surrounded by temples and shrines, is beautiful during the winter months. You can enjoy a peaceful boat ride or simply sit by the ghats and watch the sunset reflect on the water. It’s a perfect spot for photography and reflection before you head back into the city’s bustling markets to shop for traditional textiles and camel bone jewelry.

Conclusion

From the heights of the Golden Fort to the depths of the Thar Desert, the list of Things to do in Jaisalmer is as vast as the desert itself.
