Top 10 Things to Do in Jaisalmer

7 Min Read  |  Updated: Dec 2025

Welcome to Jaisalmer, the Golden City of Rajasthan, where each glimmering sandstone wall tells an age, old story of courage and tradition. In case you are scheming a get, away to this sandy haven, perhaps you are puzzling over the most excellent Things to do in Jaisalmer that will imprint your memory forever. At Tunes of Dunes, we design dream holidays that combine past, thrill and native trends so you can relish the absolute peak of Jaisalmer.

Marvel at the Living Fort of Jaisalmer

Among the top Things to do in Jaisalmer, you cannot miss the legendary Jaisalmer Fort, famously called Sonar Quila. In contrast to most forts, this is a "living fort" which is home to around ten thousand residents. Roaming its cramped lanes, seeing the age, old Jain Temples, and seeing the sunset from the bastions are some of the must, have experiences. Tunes of Dunes suggests a guided heritage walk to be able to see and understand the detailed architecture and the lively local life that is going on in the fort's golden walls.

Adventure in the Sam Sand Dunes

If you want adrenaline, definetly head to Sam Sand Dunes and experience a desert thrill. Out of all the Things to do in Jaisalmer, a 4x4 Jeep Safari followed by a traditional Camel Ride is definitely the most exciting. At Tunes of Dunes, we are all about desert adventures that get you deep into the dunes, far from the other people, where you can see the unspoiled beauty of the Thar. After the adventure, take part a cultural evening with folk music and Kalbeliya dance under the starlit sky, turning it into the highlight of your desert journey.

Discover History and Hauntings

Jaisalmers allure does not stop at the fort and sand dunes. Wandering around the Patwon Ki Haveli cluster is definitely one of the deeply cultural Things to do in Jaisalmer. The exquisite stone carvings and the gold, embellished ceilings speak volumes of the city's prosperous merchant past.

If you are gutsy, then you definitely have to see the Kuldhara abandoned village. According to the stories, the village was cursed and left empty in the dead of the night, and now it is full of ghostly remains, which happen to be quite interesting if you choose to explore with the Tunes of Dunes' knowledgeable guides answering your every question.

Peaceful Moments at Gadisar Lake

To offset the adventure, it is worth mentioning that Jaisalmer has some of the Most peaceful Things to do in Jaisalmer like a visit to Gadisar Lake. The aesthetically pleasing lake is an artificial water reservoir, which is surrounded by temples and shrines and gets its charm in the cold season. A relaxed boat trip would be an option. Otherwise, you can just sit at the steps of the riverbank and see the natures show of the sunset reflecting the water. It is an ideal place for shooting and introspection before you immerse yourself once again in the lively citys markets to buy traditional textiles and camel bone jewelry.

Conclusion

The Things to do in Jaisalmer could be numbered as 1, 500, if not more. The city has nothing less than the Golden Fort on one side and the Thar Desert on the other.
