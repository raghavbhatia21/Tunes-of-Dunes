Best Time to Visit Jaisalmer: A Complete Travel Guide [2024]

5 Min Read  |  Updated: Dec 2024

Jaisalmer, the Golden City of Rajasthan, is a mesmerizing destination known for its yellow sandstone architecture, vast sand dunes, and vibrant culture. However, being in the heart of the Thar Desert, the weather plays a crucial role in planning your trip. Here is your ultimate guide on the best time to visit Jaisalmer.

Winter (October to March) - The Peak Season

The winter months are undoubtedly the best time to explore Jaisalmer. The temperature ranges from 5째C to 24째C, making it pleasant for sightseeing and outdoor activities like the Camel Safari Jaisalmer and camping under the stars.

Highlights of visiting during Winter:

  • Perfect weather for Sam Sand Dunes safari.
  • The famous Desert Festival usually held in February.
  • Comfortable exploration of Jaisalmer Fort and Havelis.

Monsoon (July to September) - Offbeat Beauty

Jaisalmer receives very little rainfall, but the monsoon season brings humid weather. However, if you want to avoid crowds and get great deals on luxury hotels, this might be a good time. The desert landscape looks unique with patches of greenery.

Summer (April to June) - Avoid if Possible

Summers are harsh, with temperatures soaring up to 45째C - 50째C. Most outdoor activities are restricted during these months. It is generally not recommended for leisure tourism.

Things to do in Jaisalmer

Regardless of when you visit, there are plenty of things to do in Jaisalmer:

  • Visit the Jaisalmer Fort (Sonar Quila).
  • Experience prolonged Dune Bashing in a 4x4 Jeep.
  • Enjoy a cultural evening with Kalbeliya dance.
  • Explore the haunted village of Kuldhara.

Conclusion

To experience the true magic of the desert, plan your trip between October and March. Book your Jaisalmer Tour Package early, as this is the busiest season!
